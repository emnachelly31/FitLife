Cardiovascular Exercise

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity per week. Regular cardio improves heart health, boosts mood, and helps maintain a healthy weight. Activities include walking, running, cycling, and swimming.

Strength Training

Incorporate resistance training 2-3 times weekly. Building muscle increases metabolism, improves bone density, and enhances functional fitness. Focus on major muscle groups with proper form and progressive overload.

Balanced Nutrition

Fuel your body with whole foods rich in nutrients. Include lean proteins, complex carbohydrates, healthy fats, and plenty of fruits and vegetables. Stay hydrated with 8-10 glasses of water daily.

Quality Sleep

Prioritize 7-9 hours of sleep each night. Quality rest is crucial for muscle recovery, hormone regulation, and mental clarity. Establish a consistent sleep schedule and create a relaxing bedtime routine.

Mental Wellness

Practice stress management through meditation, yoga, or breathing exercises. Mental health is integral to overall fitness. Take time for activities you enjoy and maintain social connections.
